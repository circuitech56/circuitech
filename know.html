About Circuitech

Circuitech is a technology platform focused on IoT, robotics, automation, and hardware/software innovation created in 2026. The goal is to make complex systems simple, practical, and accessible for everyone.

Each project shared on the channel is supported with complete resources including circuit diagrams, source code, and step-by-step explanations.

This platform is built for learners, makers, and developers who want to build real-world systems instead of just theory.
